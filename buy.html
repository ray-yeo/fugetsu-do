3 Easy Ways to Buy Fugetsu-Do Mochi

There are also many other types and flavors offered exclusively at our store that range from more traditional pieces to new fruit flavors. We also specialize in seasonal mochi, which includes New Year's mochi for your festivals (advance ordering recommended). Select manju styles in addition to the New Year's mochi will be available during and after Christmas and in the Spring look for special Girl’s and Boy’s Day confections.

We have made our manju available for purchases online through Japansuper.com. Once on the japansuper.com website, click on "Mochi" in the left sidebar, then on either the picture or the name of the manju to order those sets online from Japansuper.com or click on the green links below to shop on their site. Shipping is only available through Japansuper.com.
