Welcome to Fugetsu-Do !



Fugetsu-Do is a family-owned mochi and manju sweet shop in the Little Tokyo area of Los Angeles, California since 1903.


Fugetsu-do creates the finest Japanese mochi and manju in Los Angeles by our expert artisans. We invite you to come by the store, visit one of the many markets that carry our products, or order our mochi online from Japansuper.com and have it delivered fresh to your doorstep!
